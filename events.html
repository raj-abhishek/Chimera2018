My mind rebels at stagnation. Give me problems, give me work, give me the most abstruse cryptogram or the most intricate analysis. I am in my own proper atmosphere, but I abhor the dull routine that exists and I crave for mental exaltation.

DO THESE WORDS RING A BELL IN YOUR MIND?

It’s time to get into his shoes, put on your hat, and grab your magnifying glass. Seek clues, put your abilities to test, and get yourself #SHERLOCKED.


Here's the ultimate satisfaction to your adventure-thirsty soul. So put on your Sherlock hats, and solve the not so difficult mystery with your wit and presence of mind.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

1. Participation – In teams of 3
2. 1st ROUND :
  • 1. If no. of teams is more than 50, It will be an MCQ round.
  • 2. 30 MCQs will have to be solved in 45 minutes.
  • 3. 50 people will be chosen from this round.
3. 2nd ROUND :
  • 1. 50 selected teams from the previous round will have to report for this round next day.
  • 2. They will be given questions in 3 fields APTITUDE, MEMORY, REASONING.
  • 3. The top 5 teams who score the highest in each one of them will be listed (5 in Aptitude, 5 in Memory, 5 in Reasoning) .
4. 3rd ROUND:
  • 1. The 5 teams will be given an extensive pictorial portraying the crime scene.
  • 2. 4 volunteers will be included in the story as suspects.
  • 3. 1 expert will guide each team through the scene
  • 4. Each team will be given 10 minutes to investigate the crime scene and 10 minutes to interrogate the witness
  • 5. They have to come to a conclusion in the next 30 (+10) minutes
  • 6. The first team to come to a conclusion will be the winner.
