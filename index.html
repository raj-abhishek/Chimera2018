About the event



CHIMERA , “An Endeavour To Think and Understand” , aims to provide a platform for the congregation of the entire medical fraternity of eastern India along with other major Universities embracing the whole country under one roof. It is beyond the dimensions of just an event it is a stage where idea meets reality.

“ Where knowledge meets rejuvenation,
And talent seeks recognition,
Stage embellished with an adroit spectra,
Respond to beckoning Chimera. ”

If you seek knowledge, ideas, thoughts and illuminating brainwaves, and indulgence in the most vitalizing events that keep the soul hungry for more - Chimera serves as a quixotic answer


The maiden edition of Chimera, which took place from the 4th to the 6th of November, 2016, saw humongous participations, primarily from the medical fraternity and various fraternities of other universities, colleges and schools making it a great success.

The huge success of the event just acted as a catalyst for us to organize it with great zeal for its second edition, this year from 8th - 10th of December. KIIT University feels extremely elated to welcome you all to this one of its kind confluence.
