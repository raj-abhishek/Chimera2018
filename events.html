Venue: To be declared soon

Discription:
Here's the ultimate satisfaction to your adventure-thirsty soul. So put on your Sherlock hats, and solve the not so difficult mystery with your wit and presence of mind.
RULES AND REGULATIONS

-Participation –:In teams of 3

1st ROUND :
  • If no. of teams is more than 50, It will be an MCQ round.
  • 30 MCQs will have to be solved in 45 minutes.
  • 50 people will be chosen from this round.
2nd ROUND :
  • 50 selected teams from the previous round will have to report for this round next day.
  • They will be given questions in 3 fields-
    • APTITUDE
    • MEMORY
    • REASONING
  • The top 5 teams who score the highest in each one of them will be listed (5 in Aptitude, 5 in Memory, 5 in Reasoning)
3rd ROUND :
  • The 5 teams will be given an extensive pictorial portraying the crime scene.
  • 4 volunteers will be included in the story as suspects.
  • 1 expert will guide each team through the scene.
  • Each time will be given 10 minutes to investigate the crime scene and 10 minutes to interrogate the witness.
  • They have to come to a conclusion in the next 30 (+10) minutes.
  • The first team to come to a conclusion will be the winner.
